How it protects you

Serious protection, built in.

Privaro is a complete, secure vault from the first photo. Every part of it is designed to keep your private moments private.

Sealed with AES-256

Every photo and video is encrypted the moment it enters your vault, along with its filename, caption, and tags.

Locked behind Face ID

Your vault opens with Face ID and a passcode only you know. The key is guarded by your device and never leaves it.

Nothing in the cloud

No account, no sync, no servers. Your vault lives on your iPhone and nowhere else, and it stays out of your backups.

A vault within a vault

Set a second passcode that opens a separate, ordinary looking decoy vault, so you never have to reveal your real one.

Decoy mode

Built for the moments that matter.

If you are ever pressured to open the app, a second passcode unlocks a believable decoy vault that looks completely normal. Your real photos stay hidden behind your real passcode, kept in a separate vault of their own.
