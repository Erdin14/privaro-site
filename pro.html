Everything Pro unlocks.

The vault is free to use. Pro lifts the limits and turns on the full security toolkit. Here is exactly what each feature does. Everything still runs on your device, encrypted, with no account.

  1. No ads, ever

    The free vault shows a single non-personalized banner on low-sensitivity screens like Settings, never inside your photos. Pro removes ads completely, everywhere in the app.

  2. Unlimited storage

    The free vault holds up to 2.5 GB. Pro removes the cap, so you can move your entire photo and video library into the vault without running out of room.

  3. Unlimited albums and folders

    Free includes up to 5 albums. Pro lets you create as many albums and nested folders as you like, so you can organize everything exactly how you want.

  4. Screenshot and recording protection

    Stops your vault from being captured by screenshots, screen recording or AirPlay while the app is open, so nothing leaks to your camera roll or to a screen mirror by accident.

  5. Break-in capture

    If someone enters the wrong passcode, Privaro can silently take a photo with the front camera and log the attempt with a timestamp, so you know if anyone has tried to get into your vault.

  6. Encrypted iCloud backup and export

    Back up your vault to your own iCloud, or export it as a single encrypted file. Both are encrypted on your device before they leave, so no one (including us) can read them. Restoring a backup is always free, even without Pro.

  1. Per-album passwords

    Put a separate code on individual albums. Even if your vault is open, your most private albums stay sealed behind their own lock.

  2. Decoy vault

    Set a second passcode that opens a believable, separate vault with its own harmless photos. If you are ever pressured to unlock, the decoy opens and your real vault stays completely hidden.

  3. Duress wipe

    Set a special passcode that, when entered, instantly and permanently erases the vault, including the decoy. For worst-case situations where the safest thing is to leave nothing behind. This cannot be undone.

  4. Wi-Fi transfer

    Send files into the vault from any computer's web browser over your local network. No cable, no cloud, and no extra app to install on your computer.

  5. Location-based lock

    Only allow the vault to unlock in places you trust, like your home. Anywhere else, it stays locked even with the right passcode.

  6. Disguise app icon

    Hide Privaro behind an everyday icon and name, like Calculator or Notes, so it does not stand out on your home screen. Color icon variants are free; the disguises are Pro.

Free or Pro, the basics never change: everything is encrypted on your device with AES-256, there is no account, and no one but you can open your vault. Pro from $9.99 per year or $14.99 once, with a 1-week free trial.
