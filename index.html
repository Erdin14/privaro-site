what's inside

Built like a vault.

Every layer runs on your iPhone. Nothing to hack remotely, because there's nothing out there to reach.

On-device encryption

Every photo, video and album is encrypted with AES-256 on your iPhone. The key never leaves your device.

Face ID and passcode

Unlock with Face ID or a private passcode separate from your phone's. It locks the moment you leave.

Decoy vault

A second passcode opens a believable decoy vault, so a passcode under pressure never reveals the real one.

Encrypted iCloud backup

Optionally back up to your own iCloud, encrypted before it leaves your device. Even we can't read it.

Hidden from screenshots

Your vault is shielded from screenshots, screen recording and AirPlay, so nothing slips out by accident.

Stealth camera

Capture photos and videos straight into the vault, so they never touch your regular camera roll.
